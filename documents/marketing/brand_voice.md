# BAUTEN Studio — Brand Voice

Tone:
- Confident, clear, friendly.
- Short sentences. No buzzwords.

Core promise:
We help companies ship beautiful, usable UI/UX fast.

Do:
- Active voice: “We deliver…”
- Use numbers when possible.
- Speed + quality + clarity.

Don’t:
- Overpromise.
- Use slang.

Tagline:
Design that ships.
